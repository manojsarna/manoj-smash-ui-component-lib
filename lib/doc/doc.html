Badges

Badges are being used to display a notification count or status information. av-badge and also add color classes according to the online status - bd-red , bd-green , bd-grey. There are different positions - bd-bottom-left bd-bottom-right bd-top-right etc. Sizes vary according to the badge - bd-xxl bd-xl bd-l bd-md
